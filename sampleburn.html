BURNS

  • Cool the Burn: Run cool (not cold) water over the burn for 10-15 minutes.
  • Remove Constrictive Items: Remove jewelry or tight clothing.
  • Protect the Burn: Cover loosely with a sterile bandage or clean cloth.
  • Manage Pain: Use over-the-counter pain relievers.
  • Monitor for Infection: Watch for signs of infection and seek help if needed.
  • High severity: Use emergency button
