SNAKE BITE

  • Stay Calm: Panic can worsen the situation by increasing heart beat
  • Keep the Affected Limb Immobilized
  • Gently clean the bite area with soap and water if available
  • Maintain the victim's body temperature to prevent hypothermia
  • Do not try to suck out the venom by mouth and do not apply ice or a tourniquet.
  • High severity: Use emergency button
